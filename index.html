About

Hi! I'm Rodrigo Silva. I'm 27 years old and I'm currently living in Fortaleza, Ceará since 2014 but I was born in Manaus, Amazonas. I've been fully dedicating myself to Web Design and Frontend development since June 2021, and in less than 6 months I gained several skills in Web Design and Frontend Development. I still have a lot to learn, but I'm improving day by day. I am very disciplined, proactive and I'm always looking to learn something new and challenging.

The plan for 2021 was to become a graphic designer but during my studies, I heard a lot about Web Design and UX/UI Design. That's when I got interested about this topic. And during my Web Design studies, I heard about Frontend Development. I didn't believe that I could bring my designs to life! And so I started my journey into these worlds.

At the moment you are reading this paragraph, I'm learning how to consume an API, how to use NPM and digging deeper into learning more Javascript. Next year, I plan to study React, Vue.js, PHP and SQL/MySQL.
